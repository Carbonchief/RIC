Set in the popular Northriding suburb, the tranquil “modern meets country” off plan development Ascot Manor will be situated. Phase 1 will consist of 27 sectional – title, 3 & 4 bedroom duplex, freestanding homes, perfect for a family.

Aesthetics as well as functionality will play integral parts in the overall remarkability of this development, in that making Ascot Manor pleasing on the eye as well as comfortable to live and entertain in.

Furthermore, in aiding with creating a more sustainable future for our clients, Bosch appliances will be installed complete with a gas hob for cooking, and the development will be have solar geysers to heat water for bathing.

The homes will be rounded off with stylish Hans Grohe sanitaryware, vinyl flooring and large gardens. This whole package comes to you in a state –of –the –art secure lifestyle estate.
